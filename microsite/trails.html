Trails

  • Bear Cave Trail: A short 0.25-mile trail featuring massive sandstone boulders and narrow passageways reminiscent of a cave.
  • Rock House Cave Trail: An easy 0.25-mile walk leading to a large rock shelter adorned with ancient Native American pictographs.
  • Cedar Falls Trail: A 2-mile round-trip trail descending into Cedar Creek Canyon to view the 95-foot Cedar Falls, one of Arkansasâ€™s most spectacular waterfalls.
  • Seven Hollows Trail: A 4.5-mile loop showcasing diverse geological features, including a natural bridge, grotto, and unique rock formations.
  • Cedar Creek Trail: A moderate 1.25-mile loop that meanders along Cedar Creek, offering views of the stream, bluffs, and lush vegetation.
  • Canyon Trail: A 1-mile trail that follows Cedar Creek through the canyon, connecting Cedar Falls Trail to the Blue Hole area.
